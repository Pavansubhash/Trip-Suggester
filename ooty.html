Local Visiting Places near Ooty

Doddabetta Peak

Budget: Low

Description: Doddabetta Peak is the highest mountain in the Nilgiri Hills, standing at an elevation of 2,637 meters. It offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Visitors can reach the peak by car or hike through the beautiful forest trails.

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

Budget: Medium

Description: The Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist attraction in Ooty. It is a scenic toy train ride that takes you through the picturesque Nilgiri Mountains, offering stunning views of tea plantations, tunnels, and bridges. The train journey from Ooty to Coonoor is particularly renowned.

Ooty Botanical Gardens

Budget: Low

Description: The Ooty Botanical Gardens is a beautifully landscaped garden spread over 55 acres. It is home to a wide variety of exotic plants, flowers, and trees. Visitors can stroll through the garden, enjoy the colorful flowerbeds, and explore the fossilized trees dating back millions of years.

Pykara Lake and Waterfalls

Budget: Low

Description: Pykara is a picturesque lake located about 20 kilometers from Ooty. It is surrounded by lush greenery and offers boating facilities for visitors. The Pykara Waterfalls, located nearby, cascade down in multiple steps, creating a mesmerizing sight and providing a refreshing experience.

Ooty Rose Garden:

Budget: Low

Description: The Ooty Rose Garden, also known as the Government Rose Garden, is the largest rose garden in India. It spans across 10 acres and features more than 20,000 varieties of roses. Visitors can admire the colorful blooms, take a leisurely walk, and enjoy the pleasant aroma of the roses.

Local Visiting Places in Goa

Calangute Beach

Budget: Medium

Description: Calangute Beach is one of the most popular and largest beaches in Goa. It offers a vibrant atmosphere with numerous shacks, water sports activities, and a bustling nightlife. Visitors can relax on the sandy shores, enjoy water sports like parasailing and jet skiing, and indulge in local Goan delicacies.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

Budget: Low

Description: The Basilica of Bom Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the oldest churches in Goa. It houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier, a revered Christian saint. The church is known for its baroque architecture and exquisite interiors.

Dudhsagar Falls

Budget: High

Description: Dudhsagar Falls is a magnificent waterfall located on the Mandovi River. It is one of the tallest waterfalls in India, surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain. Visitors can enjoy the stunning views, take a refreshing dip in the natural pools, and embark on adventurous treks in the surrounding area.

Fort Aguada

Budget: Low

Description: Fort Aguada is a well-preserved 17th-century Portuguese fort located on the Sinquerim Beach. It offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and houses a lighthouse. Visitors can explore the fort's ramparts, visit the lighthouse, and learn about its historical significance.

Anjuna Flea Market

Budget: Low

Description: Anjuna Flea Market is a famous open-air market held every Wednesday in Anjuna. It is a vibrant hub for shopping where visitors can find a variety of items, including clothes, accessories, handicrafts, and souvenirs. The market also offers live music, food stalls, and a lively atmosphere.

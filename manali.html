Local Visiting Places near Manali

Marhi

Budget: Low

Description: Marhi is a beautiful hillside located on the way to Rohtang Pass. It offers mesmerizing views of snow-capped mountains and is known for adventure activities like skiing and paragliding.

Solang Valley

Budget: Medium

Description: Solang Valley is a popular tourist spot known for its picturesque landscapes and adventure sports. Visitors can indulge in activities like skiing, paragliding, zorbing, and cable car rides.

Rahala Falls

Budget: Low

Description: Rahala Falls is a breathtaking waterfall located on the way to Rohtang Pass. It is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a peaceful atmosphere for nature lovers.

Jogini Falls

Budget: Low

Description: Jogini Falls is a stunning waterfall that requires a short trek from Vashisht village. It is a serene place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Hadimba Temple

Budget: Low

Description: Hadimba Temple is a famous religious site dedicated to the goddess Hadimba, located amidst cedar forests. It showcases unique architecture and is surrounded by tranquil surroundings.

Local Visiting Places in Munnar

Eravikulam National Park

Budget: Medium

Description: Eravikulam National Park is a wildlife sanctuary located in the Western Ghats. It is known for its rich biodiversity and is home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Visitors can explore the park's lush green landscapes, go trekking, and spot various species of flora and fauna.

Tea Gardens

Budget: Low

Description: Munnar is renowned for its vast stretches of tea plantations. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll through the tea gardens, learn about the tea-making process, and enjoy panoramic views of the rolling hills. Some tea gardens also offer guided tours and tea tasting sessions.

Mattupetty Dam

Budget: Low

Description: Mattupetty Dam is a popular tourist spot situated near Munnar. It offers a picturesque view of the surrounding hills and a serene atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy boating in the reservoir, take a scenic walk along the dam, and spot elephants and other wildlife in the vicinity.

Top Station

Budget: Low

Description: Top Station is the highest point in Munnar and offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. It is famous for its misty landscapes and panoramic vistas. Visitors can enjoy the cool climate, capture stunning photographs, and witness the beauty of the sunrise and sunset.

Echo Point

Budget: Low

Description: Echo Point is a scenic spot known for its natural echo phenomenon. Visitors can shout out their names or clap their hands to hear the sound echo back from the surrounding hills. The picturesque surroundings, lush greenery, and serene lake make it a popular attraction in Munnar.

Places to Visit in Mysuru

Mysore Palace

Budget: Medium

Description: Also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, Mysore Palace is a magnificent architectural marvel and the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty. It showcases a blend of Indo-Saracenic and traditional Dravidian architectural styles.

Chamundi Hills

Budget: Low

Description: Located on the outskirts of Mysuru, Chamundi Hills is home to the revered Chamundeshwari Temple. It offers panoramic views of the city and is a popular spot for religious and nature enthusiasts.

Brindavan Gardens

Budget: Low

Description: Situated adjacent to the Krishnarajasagara Dam, Brindavan Gardens is a sprawling terrace garden famous for its symmetric design, musical fountains, and illuminated dancing waters in the evening.

Mysore Zoo

Budget: Low

Description: The Mysore Zoo, officially known as the Sri Chamarajendra Zoological Gardens, is one of the oldest and well-maintained zoos in India. It is home to a diverse range of animals, birds, and reptiles.

St. Philomena's Church

Budget: Low

Description: Built-in neo-Gothic style, St. Philomena's Church is a majestic Roman Catholic church in Mysuru. It is dedicated to Saint Philomena and features beautiful stained glass windows and tall spires.

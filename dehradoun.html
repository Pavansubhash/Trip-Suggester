Local Visiting Places near Dehradun

Robber’s Cave

Budget: Low

Description: Robber's Cave is a popular picnic spot located near Dehradun. It features a natural cave formation with a stream flowing through it, offering a unique and adventurous experience.

Assan Barrage

Budget: Low

Description: Assan Barrage is a reservoir on the outskirts of Dehradun. It is an ideal place for birdwatching, boating, and enjoying the scenic beauty of the surrounding hills and water body.

Mussoorie

Budget: Medium

Description: Mussoorie is a popular hill station near Dehradun, known for its picturesque beauty, pleasant weather, and stunning views of the Himalayas. It offers various attractions and outdoor activities for tourists.

Sahastradhara

Budget: Low

Description: Sahastradhara, meaning "Thousand-fold Spring," is a famous tourist spot known for its natural beauty and therapeutic value. It features a waterfall and caves with sulfur springs.

Malsi Deer Park

Budget: Low

Description: Malsi Deer Park is a zoological garden and park in Dehradun. It houses various species of deer, along with other animals, and offers a peaceful environment for nature lovers.
